Week 9 Reflection:

Monday, May 5, 2025 & Wednesday May 7, 2025

I attended my consultation session with Jessie on Wednesday. During the session, Jessie and I discussed my progress and how to overcome my struggle with structuring the video. I was given advice to go through each video on Premiere Pro and decide whether I will keep or remove it from the video, as well as printing still images from the videos and placing them in front of me similar to a pin board or mood board and piece the scenes together using still images. I was also recommended to choose a soundtrack prior to structuring the video, which was very helpful. I took all feedback and advice on board, proceeding with composing a soundtrack prior to structuring the video.
