Week 6 Reflection:

Monday, April 7, 2025 & Wednesday April 9, 2025

This Monday we continued working on our Assignment 2 in class. At this stage I was going through the footage I had taken on the 21st of March. My footage was quite jumbled and unorganised, lacking direction and a clear theme. I had taken a range of different paintings however they were Impressionism focused, which I initially believed would suit my concept. However, as I continued to contemplate deeper, I went past Impressionism and changed my concept focus towards Aestheticism, which I had also mentioned in my Initial Proposal. Aestheticism became well suited, as it is the art movement of beauty over meaning, the appearance and value of beauty holding a higher importance than the meaning or sentiment behind the art. On the 9th of April I reached this conclusion and decision and planned to film a second time on the 11th of April. Previously in my first filming session I used my Canon EOS 600D DSLR camera, however due to the resolution, the video showing coloured lines due to issue with matching the lighting frequency and not meeting my expectations or likings, I decided to borrow the Sony PXW-X70 from RMIT.
On Wednesday we had a guest speaker, the founder of Silky Jazz Films, Antuong Nguyen, talk about his journey in the field of Digital Media and Film Production. It was wonderful to see his highly impressive productions and commercials, which were insightful and inspiring to witness.
