The theory was focused on Soundtrack and what is a soundtrack made of. A soundtrack is made up of, sound effects, voices and music. The sound effects include, background/ambient sounds, foley/sounds of actions on screen and designed/sound not from nature which is made up. Voices include, dialogue or any other voiced sounds. Additionally, music includes, diegetic source which are sounds existing within the world of the film and the score, which is music written specifically to accompany the video, an emotional translation of music.
During Monday's class we went through our answers to the Soundtrack Prompt which was: Annotate a 2 minute piece of video. It can be anything: video art, a segment of a film or television show, an advert, something online - any piece of moving image with a soundtrack. Take note of the following: - the number of sources of sound in the video - what are these different sources? - what purpose each type of sound plays in the video - how does sound help convey the meaning of the video - how does sound create the tone - anything else interesting you notice about how the soundtrack works with the image in your sample.

(https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t7YAuWOQUT4) Interstellar - Cornfield Chase scene by Christopher Nolan (Soundtrack by Hans Zimmer).

I have chosen the Cornfield Chase scene from Interstellar as Hans Zimmer is one of my favourite composers, as well as the film. There are around 4 sources of sound in this scene however each sound gradually enters at different times. Evidently, there are poetic connotations that align with this idea of time and the way it is placed parallel with the soundtrack. The scene begins quite slowly, the soundtrack is low yet it is present giving the viewer a small sense that something is bound to happen. Following, there is a sudden onset of suspense, with a buildup towards the edge of a cliff which we as viewers did not expect would happen. The scene encapsulates how the characters are chasing time, the soundtrack exemplifies the tension and its melody is somewhat similar to steps or a clock. Each sound, the car driving through the cornfield, the plane flying above and the soundtrack all bring the suspense of the moment into close proximity.
